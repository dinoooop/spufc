About Us

Cullen & Co is a family owned and operated WA business. Handed from father to son, we built our business, our reputation from the ground up over the past 20 years. We have decades of building, project management and problem-solving experience.

Since taking over the business in 2013, Anthony has added his own style and created a solid, reliable and quality transformation team to be able to bring your renovation dream to reality.

We understand and appreciate the trust placed in us to transform your precious home. We offer a seamless, transparent renovation experience.

Our commitment is to quality design, workmanship and customer service.

The Team

Why Choose Us

Our Commitment

At Cullen & Co we pride ourselves on offering first class renovations at an affordable price. We are committed to excellence in design, workmanship and customer service.

We have a 90% word of mouth referral rate. Our customers ask us back again and again to renovate different rooms in their home.

We limit the number of jobs we take each year. This allows the Cullen & Co team to provide the best quality and service possible.

What To Expect When We are On-site

The Cullen & Co team recognise that for you this is not a worksite, but your home. We will keep your house tidy and accessible, so you can carry on living. We treat our clients’ homes as our own. We tidy up every day to minimising the spread of dust.

Our attention to detail means doing things at break-neck speed is not our number one priority. We are conscious of completing work within the timeframe agreed to reduce the effect on your lifestyle.

Not Sure What You Want or Where to Start?

Decades of experience means we know how to best utilise the space you have, within your budget. Ask us for tips on how to renovate your home. We will provide honest, relevant advice.

We also have an interior designer on hand to help with the more exotic needs of our clientele. We bring your renovation design to life.

From start to completion, we will provide updates and information, so every base is covered.

Each renovation project is unique and gets the attention it deserves. Anthony personally oversees all works done at every job, he Project Manages each venture and he works closely with his trades to ensure quality is maintained.

The Team

Anthony

Anthony is the owner-operator of Cullen & Co. He started working in the industry 14 years ago.

He is a self-proclaimed perfectionist and is very passionate about his work.

Anthony enjoys training in the gym every day and plays beach volleyball each week.

Michael

With over 30 years of experience in the renovation industry, his knowledge will ensure you get the final product that you have dreamed off.

Michael passed down the family business to his son Anthony back in 2011 to focus on building a glazing company. Specialising in glazing, Michael runs the glazing division of the business and in his spare time loves baking pies for the family.

Jarryd

As Anthony’s right-hand man, Jarryd has been trained as a tiler and renovator since he was 19.

He’s a conscientious young man and has the care and passion passed down from Anthony and takes pride in the work he does.

You’re in safe hands when Jarryd is on the job and loves his tea and biscuits when on site.

Caleb

Caleb is our glazing apprentice, but you wouldn’t know it with his skill and capability in glazing.

He is a year away from graduating but already has the talents of a full tradesmen.

He is hardworking and is not afraid of a challenge. As a comic book buff, he’s an encyclopedia of knowledge when it comes to anything comics.
